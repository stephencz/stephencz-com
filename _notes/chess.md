Chess is a board game which has been in existence, in some form or another, for over a century.
It is a game of pure strategy. 
There is no luck involved.
The game pits two players against one another.
Each player has sixteen pieces, a king, a queen, two rooks, two bishops, two knights, and eight pawns, each of which move according to special rules.
The objective of the game is to capture the other player's king.
The first player to capture the other play's king wins the game.

### Objective and Setup

Within the game of chess there are six types of pieces: [The King](#the-king), [Queen](#the-queen), [Rook](#the-rook),
 [Bishop](#the-bishop), [Knight](#the-knight), and [Pawn](#the-pawn).

* King x 1
* Queen x 1
* Rook x 2
* Bishop x 2
* Knight x 2
* Pawn x 8

The objective of a game of chess is to capture the other player's king.
At the start of a game of chess each player will have one king, one queen, two rooks, two bishops, two knights, and eight pawns.
It is traditional for White to start the game.
